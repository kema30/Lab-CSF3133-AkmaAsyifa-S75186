Welcome to UMT

Universiti Malaysia Terengganu (UMT) is a leading public university located on the east coast of Peninsular Malaysia. The university excels in marine science, environmental studies, ocean engineering, and fisheries technology.

Faculties at UMT

Faculty Programs Offered

Faculty of Ocean Engineering Technology Ocean Engineering, Naval Architecture
Faculty of Fisheries & Food Science Aquaculture, Food Technology

About UMT

UMT began as a branch of Universiti Putra Malaysia before becoming a full university. It is recognized for its marine-focused research and academic excellence.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To be a leader in marine-focused education and research.

Mission: To advance knowledge and produce graduates who contribute to the nation and the global community.
